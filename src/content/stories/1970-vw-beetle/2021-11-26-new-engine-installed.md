Installing the new engine was more difficult than removing the old one.
Lining up the mounting bolts with the transaxle spleens was not easy.
Then, I discovered that I did not properly align the clutch plate!
I had to drop the new engine, realign the clutch, and reinstall it.
Eventually, I got it in and running.
