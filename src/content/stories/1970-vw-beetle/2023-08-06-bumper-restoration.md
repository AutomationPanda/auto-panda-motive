The Beetle's bumpers were clearly not original.
They were too light and too thin.
They also had a bit of surface rust on the fronts and backs.
I cleaned off the rust with superfine steel wool.
Then, I painted the backsides with rust protection.
Next, I polished the chrome to make it really shine.
Finally, I used high quality electrical tape for that black line across the bumpers.
