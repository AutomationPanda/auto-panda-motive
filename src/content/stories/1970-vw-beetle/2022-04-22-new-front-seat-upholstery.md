The seat upholstery from the interior kit arrived next. 
I stripped the front seats down to their metal frames.
Then, it's a layer of burlap followed by the "seat buns" and finally the vinyl covers.
The two-tone colors looked amazing.
The only downside was that the classic "VW smell" went away with the old coconut fiber seat pads.
