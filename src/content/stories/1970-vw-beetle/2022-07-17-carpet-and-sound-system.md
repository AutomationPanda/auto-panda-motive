The sound system has a Bluetooth head unit, an amplifier in the trunk,
6" Focal speakers with tweeters in the front kickpanels,
6x9" Focal woofers mounted to the back shelf,
and an 8" Rockford Fosgate P300-8P Punch subwoofer hidden under the back shelf.
I had to run wires up and down the cabin.

The carpet kit had pre-cut pieces and carpet pads.
I chose a brown loop carpet to complement the tan color palette.
From top to bottom, the colors go from light to dark:
The sand tweed headliner, the tan upholstery, and the deep brown carpet.
When it all came together, the interior looked _fresh_.
