Dropping the engine was the biggest car project I had undertaken up to this time.
I had to disconnect the fuel line, the air hoses, the heater boxes, the wires, and the tray.
When dropping it, I lowered it onto a custom dolly I built myself.
I felt so proud of myself once it was out because I proved that I could do it.
