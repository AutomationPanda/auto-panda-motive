Two weeks after buying the Beetle,
the engine overheated while driving it home from church.
Smoke was billowing out of the back.
By the time I pulled off the highway into a gas station, the engine was seething.
I had to tow it home.

This moment was my "call to adventure" as a car guy.
I tried finding shops to fix the engine, but nobody in my area could do it.
Ultimately, I had to buy a new engine and swap it myself.
I had never done major car projects like this before.
Now, there was no choice: I had to rise to the challenge.
