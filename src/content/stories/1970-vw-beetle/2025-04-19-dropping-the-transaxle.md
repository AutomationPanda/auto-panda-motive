At the end of 2024, the transmission when "boom".
It could not shift into gears 3 and 4.
When I tried, the transmission behaved as if it were in neutral.
Unfortunately, I didn't have time to repair it.
I had to work on my [Karmann Ghia](/cars/1974-karmann-ghia) instead.
Once I had time, I dropped the engine and removed the transaxle.
I thought I could open up the transaxle to fix it,
but I quickly realized that the job was more than I could handle myself.
