After ruling out other possible causes,
I suspected that the backfire may have been caused by an air leak in the exhaust system.
Sure enough, when I disconnected the exhaust system,
I found exactly where the air leaks happened.
The gaskets around the connection ports had thick black soot.
This was the smoking gun I needed to find.
