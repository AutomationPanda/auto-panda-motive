The Beetle's interior needed to be redone.
The previous owner was a smoker, so the interior smelled like cigarettes.
The yellow vinyl upholstery and old door panels looked ugly, too.
Plus, I wanted to install a full sound system that would have required me to rip up the carpet anyway.

Installing a whole new interior was a massive project.
I had already started it with the door panels and the seat upholstery.
Now, it was time to do the carpet and the headliner.
I ripped out everything down to the bare metal.
I even had to remove the rear glass and the front windshield.
