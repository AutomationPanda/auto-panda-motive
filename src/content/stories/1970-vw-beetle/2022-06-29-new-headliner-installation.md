I ordered a sand-colored tweed headliner to complement the paint,
the new tan upholstery, and new the brown carpet.
I installed Hushmat along the roof followed by a headliner pad with spray glue.
Then, to install the headliner, I had to mount its "ribs" and stretch it across the roof.
I needed to use edge trim to hold the headliner in place around the windshields and windows.
The effort was worthwhile: the headliner looked _nice_ once it was done.
