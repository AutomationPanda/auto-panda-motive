[Volkzbitz](https://volkzbitz.com/) is the place to go if you want a refurbished Solex carburator instead of a questionable aftermarket carb.
I had tuning problems with occasional backfires,
so I thought a new(ish) carburetor might best.
Volkzbitz carbs are works of art.
I got them for both my Beetle and my [Karmann Ghia](/cars/1974-karmann-ghia).
As someone once told me, "Buy once, cry once."
