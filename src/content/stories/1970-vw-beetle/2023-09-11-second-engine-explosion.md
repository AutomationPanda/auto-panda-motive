The Beetle had a backfire that progressively worsened.
I tried everything to find it, but I could not locate it.
Unfortunately, on this day, the backfire got so bad that it literally blew up the engine.
It would not start again.
It happened about a mile from my house, and I had to push it home.
The Beetle would be off the road until after Christmas.
