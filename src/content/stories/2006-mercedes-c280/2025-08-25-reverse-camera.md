The new Android radio unit came with a connection for a reverse camera.
I mounted a camera to the trunk lid in the slot for the right license plate light.
I routed the line from the radio through the front dash, under the trim over the doors,
and behind the backseat through to the trunk.
Now, it's easier to parallel park!
