Replacing the rear subframe was one of the most intense car projects I've ever undertaken.
I had to disconnect so many parts:
wheels, ABS, parking brake, differential, and even the muffler.
Uncle Jackson had to help me cut through the exhaust system connecting bolts that completely seized.
I carefully dropped the whole subframe assembly with the differential
onto the engine dolly I originally built for my VW Beetle's engine.
