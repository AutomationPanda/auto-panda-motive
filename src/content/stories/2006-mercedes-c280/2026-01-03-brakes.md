The brakes had more life in them, but I wanted to replace them for a few reasons:

1. The rotors were wavy and needed to be replaced soon.
2. I wanted to switch from semi-metallic pads to ceramics to limit the brake dust.
3. I wanted to paint the calipers red to make them pop.

It made sense to do the whole job at once.
I bought Zimmermann Coat Z rotors and Akebono brake pads.
For the caliper paint, I bought the G2 High Temperature Brake Caliper Paint System.
All these parts proved to be good choices.
