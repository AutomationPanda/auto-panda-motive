The stock side mirror indicators looked a little to "bling" for my taste.
I wanted indicators with a slightly more subtle look.
I found a pair that had a smoked look.
When the turn signals are activated, these indicator don't just blink: they animate a line.
They complement the new LED headlights perfectly.
