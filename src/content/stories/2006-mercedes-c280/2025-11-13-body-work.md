Luis and the team at [Solo Blend](https://soloblend.wixsite.com/website)
replaced the front doors with the ones I pulled from the junkyard.
They also banged out the baseball-sized dents from the hood.
Then, they painted the panels and blended them to match.
Since I don't do body work myself, I was thankful to find this shop nearby.
They did an awesome job for a reasonable price.
I also got to see Luis's classic VW Beetle!
