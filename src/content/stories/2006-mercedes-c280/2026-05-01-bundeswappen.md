The *Bundeswappen* is the [coat of arms of Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coat_of_arms_of_Germany).
I thought it would be a cool sticker for the rear window,
especially since the car was made in Bremen.
I chose a white sticker rather than the German tri-color (black, red, gold)
to better match the car's black theme.
