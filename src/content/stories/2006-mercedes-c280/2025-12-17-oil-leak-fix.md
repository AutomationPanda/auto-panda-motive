For the longest time, the engine had a small oil leak.
I traced it down to one of the expansion plugs on the back side of the engine.
The fix was thankfully easy.
It turned out that the old expansion cap was missing two of its teeth!
Seeing that gave me full confidence that a new cap would surely fix the oil leak.
