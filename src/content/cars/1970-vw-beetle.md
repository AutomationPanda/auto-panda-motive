The VW Beetle is arguably the most beloved kind of car in automotive history.
It is a car that immediately recognized by folks young and old.
Despite being a rarity on the roads today, kids still play "punch buggy" whenever they spot one.

I've loved the Beetle since I was a boy.
I remember playing with diecast toy Beetles that pull back and launch forward
while watching the old _Herbie_ Disney movies.
I never imagine that I'd own my own classic Beetle one day.

I bought this VW Beetle during COVID.
My wife was the one who encouraged me to buy it.
And, boy, this car has needed a _lot_ of projects.
After several years, it has a whole new engine, a new interior, and a lot of memories.
I even created a video to tell the story of my Bug.
It's a keynote I delivered at a software engineering conference,
but it's a great video for anyone to watch. Enjoy!
