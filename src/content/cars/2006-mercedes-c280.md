At first glance, there's nothing special about a W203.
It's a fairly ordinary Mercedes, caught between the more old-school Nineties models
and the sleeker designs of the Teens and Twenties.
But this is still a solid vehicle.
It has great handling and a smooth ride.
It was also built in the final era before electronics took over everything.

This car has been in my family for nearly its entire life.
My mom bought this car back when I was still in college.
After using it as her daily driver for several years,
she graciously gifted it to me in 2025.
I always wanted to wrench on a Euro motor that was more modern than my aircooled VWs.

This W203 feels _timeless_.
It's hard to believe this car is over 20 years old.
My goal for this build has been to modernize it while keeping its original character.
I did a lot of repairs, maintenance, and upgrades.
I'm proud of the work I put into it, and I'm thankful that it's still on the road.
I hope to keep driving it until it becomes a classic.
